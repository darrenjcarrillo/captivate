DERMAL FILLERS

HOME / BEAUTY BAR / DERMAL FILLERS

Aesthetic care isn’t limited to over-the-counter creams, and it certainly doesn’t have to involve surgery. Regarding concerns like wrinkles and facial volume loss, a healthy middle ground can be found in dermal fillers. These injectables are your minimally-invasive solution for better skin without downtime. Get in touch with our team at Captivate Wellness to learn more today.

What Are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are injectable treatments that improve facial volume and contour. The fillers we offer here are made of hyaluronic acid, which is a natural substance found in the human body. When injected, fillers increase the plumpness of the targeted area. These treatments can provide long-term improvements in lip size, cheek fullness, and more.

Dermal Filler Options

JUVÉDERM®

20-30 Minutes
1-2 Years

JUVÉDERM® fillers offer anti-aging, preventative, and facial balancing benefits. These fillers are ideal for addressing volume loss, correcting asymmetries, and softening lines and wrinkles. Injections take 20-30 minutes to perform, and results can be seen immediately. With optimal treatment, results may last 1-2 years, depending on the formula used and the area treated.

LEARN MORE

Restylane®

6-8 Months

Restylane® fillers feature unique XpresHAn Technology that allows for targeted plumping while helping you retain your full range of facial motion. This filler can also be used to treat the hands along with several areas of the face. Results last for 6-18 months, depending on the targeted area and the chosen Restylane® filler.

LEARN MORE

Kybella® Liquid Liposuction

6 Sessions
Permanent

Though not exactly a dermal filler, Kybella® is an injectable treatment focused on submental fullness. However, unlike fillers that add volume, Kybella® works to reduce it. Kybella® features synthetic deoxycholic acid, which helps break down and absorbtion of fat. When injected under the jaw, it can help reduce the appearance of a double chin. Typically, six treatment sessions, spaced at least one month apart, are needed. However, results are permanent (so long as you maintain a proper diet).
